Mission

Our mission is to reduce financial, technological, and educational barriers to secure communication over the Internet.

History

ISRG was founded in May of 2013 in order to serve as the legal entity for the Let's Encrypt certificate authority. The group's first directors were Josh Aas and Eric Rescorla. The group's founding sponsors and partners were Mozilla, the Electronic Frontier Foundation, the University of Michigan, Cisco, and Akamai.

Structure

ISRG is a California public benefit corporation, and is recognized by the IRS as a tax-exempt organization under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Our EIN is 46-3344200.
