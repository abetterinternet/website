									<h3><span style="color:#003a70;">Intent to End OCSP Service</span></h3>

									<p>Let’s Encrypt is planning to <strong><a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/07-23-replacing-ocsp-with-crls/2lkgv/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%" style="color: rgb(55, 113, 200);">end OCSP support</a></strong> in favor of Certificate Revocation Lists (CRLs) to better protect user privacy and streamline our internal infrastructure. Unlike OCSP, CRLs do not expose user IP addresses which helps mitigate significant privacy concerns.&nbsp;<br>
									<br>
									This change will also free up considerable Let’s Encrypt resources that will soon be better spent on other aspects of our operations. <strong><a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/07-23-replacing-ocsp-with-crls/2lkgv/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%" style="color: rgb(55, 113, 200);">Read more</a></strong></p>
