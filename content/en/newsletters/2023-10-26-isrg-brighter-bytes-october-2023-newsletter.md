									<h3>Securing Our World</h3>

									<p>This October is the 20th anniversary of Cybersecurity Awareness Month! In recognition of this milestone anniversary, CISA announced a new cybersecurity awareness program, <a href="cisa.gov/secure-our-world">Secure Our World</a>.</p>

									<h3>Global Encryption Day</h3>
									October 21st was <a href="twitter.com/torproject/status/1715870568816542090">#GlobalEncryptionDay</a>: an annual day of action organized across the web by the <a href="globalencryption.org/">Global Encryption Coalition</a>, when civil society organizations, businesses, technologists, and millions of Internet users across the world come together to promote, protect, and defend strong encryption. The world is a safer place with Let's Encrypt providing free TLS certificates, but we still have room to grow - nearly 20% of global page loads are still unsecured today.</td>

									<h3>(Re)Introducing Hickory DNS</h3>

									<p>Our Prossimo project's Trust-DNS initiative is now known as Hickory DNS!<br>
									The name change will happen during the move of the project into the Hickory DNS organization on GitHub. This transition to an organization and away from founder and maintainer Benjamin Fry's personal GitHub account will allow for a greater set of administrative options for other collaborators like Dirkjan Ochtman, who's been helping maintain the project. Learn more about it in this <a href="memorysafety.org/blog/announcing-hickory-dns/">blog post</a> written by Benjamin Fry.</p>

									<h3>Secure by Design</h3>

									<p>The goal of our Prossimo project is to help make the Internet more secure by moving its critical infrastructure to memory safe code. So it's great to see that memory safety is a centerpiece of CISA's updated <a href="cisa.gov/securebydesign">Secure by Design white paper</a>! You'll find it listed as a top Secure-by-Design Tactic.&nbsp;</p>

									<h3>Bringing Privacy-Respecting Telemetry to Human Rights Defenders</h3>

									<p>We're excited to announce our Divvi Up project's first production user - Horizontal! <a href="wearehorizontal.org/index">Horizontal</a> is a nonprofit ​​organization supporting frontline defenders, activists, and journalists through digital security and tool development. We love that they're bringing privacy-respecting telemetry to their work at the intersection of technology, human rights, and justice. Learn more about this partnership in our <a href="divviup.org/blog/horizontal/">blog post</a>.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

									<h3>Divvi Up and Firefox</h3>

									<p>We recently shared on our <a href="divviup.org/blog/divvi-up-in-firefox/">blog</a> that Mozilla will be deploying our Divvi Up privacy-preserving metrics collection technology in Firefox. As a browser that keeps its users' privacy at the forefront, Firefox is an ideal partner for an early deployment of Divvi Up. Check out the blog post, or watch a <a href="vimeo.com/873504129">short video</a> about our partnership!</p>

									<h3>Learning from Let's Encrypt's Ten Years of Success</h3>

									<p>Ten years ago, ISRG was founded to tackle the issue of web security. Through Let's Encrypt, we streamlined and automated the cert issuance process, and since then we've issued over 4 billion certs and secured more than 360 million&nbsp;websites. Check out this great <a href="infoworld.com/article/3708294/learning-from-lets-encrypts-10-years-of-success.html">article</a> about our organization's growth and change by Matt Asay at InfoWorld.</p>

									<h3>Consider a Sponsorship!</h3>

									<p>Many organizations are finalizing their 2024 budgets right around this time. If your organization uses or benefits from Let's Encrypt, please consider <a href="abetterinternet.org/sponsor/">becoming a sponsor</a>! Sponsorships provide the majority of our funding, not only for the continued work of Let's Encrypt, but also for projects like Prossimo and Divvi Up. And if you're already a sponsor, then we say thank you for helping us realize our mission of a more secure and privacy-respecting Internet.</p>
