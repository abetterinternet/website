									<h3><span style="color:#003a70;">We Issued Our First Six Day Cert</span></h3>
									Earlier this year we <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/2025-01-16-6-day-and-ip-certs-/34n35/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">announced</a> our intention to introduce short-lived certificates with lifetimes of six days as an option for our subscribers. Yesterday we issued our first short-lived certificate. You can see the certificate at the bottom of our post linked below. We issued it to ourselves and then immediately revoked it so we can observe the certificate’s whole lifecycle. This is the first step towards making short-lived certificates available to all subscribers.<br>

									<h3><span style="color:#003a70;">New ISRG email newsletters now available.&nbsp;</span>​​​​​</h3>
									Last month we <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/opt-in-/34n3c/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">launched a handful of new emails you can subscribe to</a> if you'd like to stay up to date on Let's Encrypt technical updates and/or the latest from our Prossimo and Divvi Up projects.

									<h3><span style="color:#003a70;">Stay on top of cert expiration, if you need it.&nbsp;</span>​​​​​</h3>
									For those who would like to continue receiving expiration notification emails when your certs are set to expire, we recommend using a third party service such as <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/lse-platform-certificates-lite/34n3g/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">Red Sift Certificates Lite</a> (formerly Hardenize). Red Sift’s monitoring service providing expiration emails is free of charge for up to 250 certificates. More monitoring options can be found <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/docs-monitoring-options-/34n3k/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">here</a>.

									<h3><span style="color:#003a70;">A community here for you.</span>​​​​​</h3>
									If you're new to Let's Encrypt or have hit a technical question you can't quite figure out, remember that the <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/2025-02-20/34n3n/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">Let's Encrypt Community</a> is here to help! You can sign up for specific updates, follow various categories, or post a question as you need.&nbsp;</td>

									<h3><span style="color:#003a70;">Encryption for Everybody</span>​​​​​</h3>
									2025 marks ten years of Let’s Encrypt. Already this year we’ve taken steps to continue to deliver on our values of user privacy, efficiency, and innovation, all with the intent of continuing to deliver free TLS certificates to as many people as possible; to deliver encryption for everybody.<br>
									<br>
									And while we’re excited about the technical progress we’ll make this year, we’re also going to celebrate this tenth anniversary by highlighting the people around the world who make our impact possible. It’s no small village. <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/2-14-encryption-for-everybody-/34n3r/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">Take a read through our first post celebrating ten years of Let's Encrypt.</a><br>
									&nbsp;
									<h3><span style="color:#003a70;">Join our global community of sponsors!</span>​​​​​</h3>
									For the last decade, Let's Encrypt has provided free and user-friendly TLS certificates, facilitating exponential growth in Internet encryption worldwide. Now serving more than <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/stats-/34n3v/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%" style="color: rgb(55, 113, 200);">half a billion domains</a>, we continue to create a more secure and privacy-respecting Web for the billions of people worldwide who use it.<br>
									<br>
									This impact is made possible in part by less than 100 organizations who sponsor Let’s Encrypt. If you work for a company or organization that benefits from Let's Encrypt, consider joining organizations like <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/sponsors-/34n4n/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%" style="color: rgb(55, 113, 200);">Siteground, HaProxy, SecureW2, and more</a> by coming on board as a sponsor. <strong>100% of the funding for Let’s Encrypt comes from sponsorships and donations.</strong><br>
