<h3><span style="font-size:20px;"><span style="color:#003a70;">DNS-PERSIST-01: A New Model for DNS-based Challenge Validation </span></span></h3>
<span style="font-size:16px;">DNS-PERSIST-01 introduces a new <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/docs-challenge-types-/452z5/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">ACME challenge type</a> based on the <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/draft-ietf-acme-dns-persist-00/452z8/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">IETF draft specification</a>. Instead of creating a new DNS record for every certificate, you can set up a persistent authorization record that handles certificate issuance and renewals. This removes DNS updates from the critical path.<br>
<br>
This is especially helpful for IoT devices, multi-tenant platforms, and scenarios requiring batch certificate operations. DNS-PERSIST-01 also supports wildcard certificates and you can authorize multiple CAs.&nbsp;<br>
<br>
Staging rollout starts in late Q1 2026, with full production expected in Q2. Check out <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/2026-02-18-dns-persist-01/452zc/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">our blog</a> to learn how this can simplify your certificate management workflow.</span><br>
<br>
<br>
<strong><span style="font-size:20px;"><span style="color:#003a70;">Security Researchers Try out Six-Day and IP Address Certs</span></span></strong><br>
<br>
<span style="font-size:16px;">We’re glad to see members of the TLS community, including Ivan Ristić of Feisty Duck and Scott Helme, exploring and sharing early experiences with Let’s Encrypt’s six-day certificates and IP address certificates now that they're <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/ay-and-ip-general-availability/452zg/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">generally available</a>. Shorter lifetimes shrink the window of risk if a key is compromised, and both posts share thoughtful takes on what adoption looks like in practice. Take a look at the posts from <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/rtificates-generally-available/452zk/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">Feisty Duck</a> and <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/m-6-day-certificates-are-here-/452zn/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">Scott</a>!&nbsp;<br>
<br>
If you have an experience with our new six-day or IP address certifications, please feel free to share them on our <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/2026-02-19/452zr/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">Community Forum</a>.&nbsp;</span><br>
