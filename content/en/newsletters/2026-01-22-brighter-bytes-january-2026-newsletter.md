<h3><span style="font-size:20px;"><span style="color:#003a70;">6-day Certs For All! </span></span></h3>
<span style="font-size:16px;">Let’s Encrypt short-lived and IP address certificates are now generally available! In order to get a short-lived certificate subscribers simply need to select the ‘shortlived’ <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/docs-profiles-/42q67/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">certificate profile</a> in their ACME client.<br>
<br>
We are excited to make this type of certificate available because short-lived certificates improve security. They require more frequent validation and encourage subscribers to reduce their reliance on unreliable revocation mechanisms.&nbsp;<br>
<br>
Continue reading the <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/ay-and-ip-general-availability/42q51/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">full blog post</a> to learn more.</span><br>
<br>
<br>
<strong><span style="font-size:20px;"><span style="color:#003a70;">Look for Gen Y in Your New Certificates</span></span></strong><br>
<br>
<span style="font-size:16px;">If querying crt.sh is one of your favorite hobbies, look out for certificates from our new roots! If you’re curious about what this looks like, <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/-id-23612822160/42q54/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">here’s</a> the first cert issued from our <a href="https://outreach.abetterinternet.org/e/1011011/certs-gen-y-root-ye-txt/42q57/%%email_id%%/h/%%internal.tracker_url_hash%%">YE1</a> Root.<br>
